About the Foundation

Shalom Guru Foundation is preparing to officially begin its journey of social service, compassion, and community support in Nepal.

Who We Are

Shalom Guru Foundation is a community-focused organization committed to serving people with compassion, dignity, and hope. We are preparing to launch our programs and public work officially on Baishakh 1.

✓
Compassion
We believe service should be rooted in love, dignity, and responsibility.
✓
Community
We want to support and uplift local communities through meaningful future programs.
✓
Transparency
We want our supporters to clearly see our growth, progress, and future impact.

Mission

To serve communities with compassion, dignity, and hope by supporting education, social welfare, health awareness, environmental action, and opportunities for vulnerable people in Nepal.

Vision

A stronger, fairer, more compassionate society where every person has the opportunity to live with dignity, support, and hope.

Foundation Objectives (उद्देश्यहरू)

Shalom Guru Foundation का मुख्य उद्देश्यहरू

Provide scholarship and educational support for students who are deprived of education due to financial hardship.

आर्थिक अभावका कारण शिक्षा प्राप्त गर्न नसकेका विद्यार्थीहरूलाई छात्रवृत्ति तथा शैक्षिक सहयोग प्रदान गर्ने।
Promote environmental protection through plantation, sanitation, drainage, and public cleanliness efforts.

वृक्षारोपण, सरसफाइ, ढल व्यवस्थापन तथा वातावरण संरक्षण सम्बन्धी कार्यक्रम सञ्चालन गर्ने।
Organize health camps, clinics, and support programs for financially vulnerable people.

आर्थिक रूपमा कमजोर वर्गका लागि स्वास्थ्य शिविर तथा स्वास्थ्य सहयोग कार्यक्रम सञ्चालन गर्ने।
Conduct poverty alleviation and social welfare related programs.

गरिबी निवारण तथा सामाजिक कल्याण सम्बन्धी कार्यक्रम सञ्चालन गर्ने।
Support people affected by educational barriers, poverty, and social discrimination.

शैक्षिक, आर्थिक तथा सामाजिक विभेदबाट प्रभावित व्यक्तिहरूलाई सहयोग गर्ने।
Support children, vulnerable groups, and those needing protection and assistance.

बालबालिका तथा असहाय वर्गका व्यक्तिहरूको संरक्षण तथा सहयोग गर्ने।
Run social, economic, and cultural awareness programs for community upliftment.

सामाजिक, आर्थिक तथा सांस्कृतिक चेतनामूलक कार्यक्रम सञ्चालन गर्ने।
Provide skill-based training and help create livelihood opportunities.

सीप विकास तालिम प्रदान गरी रोजगारीका अवसर सिर्जना गर्ने।
Promote and preserve Nepali cultural values through relevant programs.

नेपाली संस्कृति तथा परम्पराको संरक्षण र प्रवर्द्धन गर्ने।
Raise awareness against social evils, injustice, violence, and harmful practices.

सामाजिक विकृति, अन्याय, हिंसा तथा कुरीतिविरुद्ध जनचेतना फैलाउने।
Promote awareness related to rights, duties, law, and human dignity.

मानव अधिकार, कर्तव्य तथा कानुनी चेतना सम्बन्धी कार्यक्रम सञ्चालन गर्ने।
